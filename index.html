Vowel Trouble


Rules:

Vowel Trouble is a one-player game.

The vowels of each word are missing.

Spell the word and press 'Enter'.

You receive 10 points if you spell the word correctly without help.

If you need help, touch the help button to see the word for a second.

Remember:  You receive fewer points each time you get help.
