•  The other student must say one word.

•  The first person to enter all the words wins!!!

Scrambler

  Scrambler Rules:

• Scrambler is a one-player game.

•  The letters in each word are scrambled (mixed up).

•  You must enter the correct spelling of the word in the box.

•  hraic is the word chair, for instance.

•  You may also ask for the spelling.

•  Add the new word in the text box and touch enter.

•  The first person to enter all the words wins!!!
